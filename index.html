Learning Resources

Explore our curated list of resources to enhance your learning experience.

GitHub

Discover a vast array of projects, libraries, and collaborative opportunities on GitHub. You can also find our organization's repositories and contribute to the BREWD Academy projects.

Hugging Face

Hugging Face is an essential platform for Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) enthusiasts. Browse models, datasets, and more to deepen your understanding of these fields.

Kaggle

Kaggle offers a platform for data science competitions, datasets, and notebooks. Engage with a community of data science practitioners and learn by doing.

Coursera

Coursera provides courses from universities and institutions around the world. Explore a variety of topics to find courses that align with your learning goals.
